DFM - DFA

DFA Benefits

DFA is a vital link between an OEM and its EMS provider to ensure successful PCB assembly. DFA’s key benefits for the OEM.

  • Clarifying and detailing important OEM design objectives upfront to avoid potential problems later in the process.
  • Exchanging new ideas for a more efficient layout.
  • Successful completion of the first article to provide proof of design concept and verify proper component placement.
  • Resolve potential assembly issues, if critical placements presents in PCB.
  • Maintaining close interaction with DFA and layout engineers to satisfactorily replace components positioning tolerance, height, and space limitations.
  • Optimizing a design by implementing different, but more efficient component positioning to make a PCB more DFA acceptable.
